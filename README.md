Hi there, I'm Skye SU 👋

I am a researcher specializing in **computational social science, NLP pipelines, and applied LLM workflows**. Coming from a background in Social Policy (MSocSc, Lingnan University), I focus on bridging domain knowledge with technical infrastructure — building automated data pipelines, RAG-based systems, and agentic workflows to extract insights from complex texts (e.g., legal judgments, policy documents, and public discourse).

**My Core Focus Areas:**
- **Applied LLM & Agentic Workflows**: Designing ReAct agents, routing structures, and source-grounded RAG systems.
- **Multilingual NLP Pipelines**: Web scraping, text preprocessing (spaCy), and semantic analysis across different jurisdictions.
- **Computational Legal Studies & AI Governance**: Turning unstructured legal/policy texts into structured, interoperable datasets.

**I am currently open to:**
- Research Assistant positions in Interdisciplinary AI Research, particularly at the intersection of LLMs and Humanities/Social Sciences (e.g., Computational Law, AIED, Computational Social Science).
- Roles involving LLM-driven research tooling and empirical text analysis.

**Recent Tooling & Infrastructure:**
